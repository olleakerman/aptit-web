Aptit

The recipe app for iOS. Import recipes from a link, a photo or text, plan the week's dinners and choose what lands on the shopping list.

Features

  • Import recipes from a link, photo or text — Aptit reads and structures them for you.
  • Weekly planning and a shared shopping list — you choose what gets bought.
  • Household sharing — invite the family with a code.
  • Fridge cleaner — get recipe ideas from what you already have at home.
  • Recipe books, favorites and smart search.
  • Home-screen widgets — see today's and the week's meals without opening the app.
