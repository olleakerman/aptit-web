FAQ

How do I import a recipe?

Tap + in the Recipes tab and choose whether to import from a link, a photo or text. Aptit reads the recipe automatically — it takes a few seconds.

The recipe import failed. What do I do?

Some sites are harder to read, especially ones that require a login or load content dynamically. Try copying the recipe text and importing from text instead, or take a photo of the recipe.

How do I invite my family to the same household?

Open your profile via the gear icon on the home screen and choose Household. There you'll find an invite code your family can enter when creating an account or via Join household.

How does Aptit Pro work?

Aptit Pro unlocks unlimited photo import, the Fridge cleaner and your own recipe books — for everyone in the household on a single subscription. You can start, pause and cancel the subscription anytime in the App Store under Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Full terms are in the Terms of Use.

How do I delete my account?

Open your profile via the gear icon on the home screen and choose Delete account. All your personal data and content are deleted within 30 days. You can also email us and we'll help you.

Where are my recipes stored?

In Google Firebase, in a data center within the EU. Only you and members of your household can see your content. Read more in the privacy policy.

Which iOS version is required?

Aptit requires iOS 16 or later.

How do I add widgets to my home screen?

Press and hold the home screen until the apps jiggle, tap the plus in the corner and search for Aptit. Choose Today or Week overview and tap Add Widget.

Requests and feedback
